About Card Dealers Inc.

Founded on a passion for the game, Card Dealers Inc. was established to bring a new level of professionalism and skill to the card dealing industry. Our team consists of seasoned professionals with decades of combined experience in casinos, high-stakes tournaments, and private events. We pride ourselves on our integrity, efficiency, and dedication to creating an unparalleled experience for every client.

Whether you're hosting a small get-together or a large corporate event, we guarantee a flawless and engaging service that will leave your guests impressed. Our mission is to not just deal cards, but to deal in excellence.
